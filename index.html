You’ll leave the ordinary world behind the minute you step into this magical sweet spot overflowing with opportunities to truly savor summer with the excitement of a wide-eyed kid. Whether it’s sampling chocolate bubbling from a fountain, soaking up the glittery ocean views from atop the Ferris wheel, discovering how nature’s sweet fruits grow and ripen, or enjoying the Fair’s golden-ticket entertainment, your toughest decision will be choosing what to indulge in first!

Live Music

We offer up some of the best local music in the city every Monday night... for free. Check out LA bands that are about to make it big (acts like Fitz & the Tantrums, Superhumanoids and even Local Natives have graced the residency stage) without spending a dime—well, except on maybe a beer, or a round for the folks onstage

Vegan Food Trucks

Check out our plant based trucks!

1.Kind Kravings is a Wichita-based vegan food truck that specializes in offering good ol‘ fashioned home-cooked comfort foods with a healthy twist.

2.V-Grits, which stands for “vegan girl raised in the South” is an all vegan food truck based in Louisville, Kentucky, and it’s been serving up plant-based comfort food since 2015

3.The Shimmy Shack food truck aims to dispel the myths that veggie food is bland and boring, and from the soothing mint green hue of the truck to the comforting, indulgent vegan fare available for purchase, we’d say they’re doing a fine job!
